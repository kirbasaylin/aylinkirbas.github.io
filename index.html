About

I'm a junior at The Ohio State University pursuing concurrent B.S. degrees in Electrical & Computer Engineering and Geographic Information Science, with a specialization in Spatial Data Analytics.

My work has traced a line through aerospace, defense innovation, healthcare data, and geospatial intelligence. I'm currently a Software Development Intern at Siemens Digital Industries in Cincinnati, OH.

Earlier this year I evaluated dual-use AI/ML and autonomous systems for the Defense Innovation Unit at Parallax Advanced Research. Before that I led science-mission design for an autonomous lunar/Mars drone through NASA L'SPACE, and built MySQL infrastructure for the National Geospatial-Intelligence Agency through AFRL.

Outside of engineering I play NCR D1-AA rugby for Ohio State, contribute to the Underwater Robotics Club, and serve in the Society of Women Engineers.
